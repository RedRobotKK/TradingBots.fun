How the 30-second refresh works

Coinzilla's Refreshable Zone rotates the creative every 30 seconds without a full page reload — only the ad iframe updates. Each rotation counts as a new impression against your CPM contract. This is the standard mechanism used by crypto media sites (CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph) to multiply impression volume without degrading UX. Coinzilla requires that the ad is in-view for ≥1 second before the refresh fires, which also protects your CPM rate (low-quality refresh impressions are discarded by their fraud filter).
